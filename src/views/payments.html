Record a Payment

Enter a cash or cheque payment against a borrower. It updates that loan’s outstanding balance automatically — you don’t need to open the loan record. Pending cheques are saved but do not reduce the balance until you mark them cleared.

Record a Charge — late fee / cheque bounce

Record a late fee or cheque-bounce charge against a borrower. This is logged on the loan and in the audit trail for your records. It does not change the borrower's outstanding balance.
