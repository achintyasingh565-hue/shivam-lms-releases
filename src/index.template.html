Install as an App

💻 On a Computer (Chrome / Edge)

  1. Open the browser menu (⋮ top-right).
  2. Choose "Install page as app" (Edge) or "Cast, save and share → Create shortcut…" (Chrome), then tick "Open as window".
  3. An icon is added to your desktop / taskbar — open it anytime like normal software.

📱 On a Phone (Android / iPhone)

  1. Open this page in Chrome (Android) or Safari (iPhone).
  2. Tap the menu / share button.
  3. Choose "Add to Home screen" — it appears as an app icon.

This app works fully offline and stores data on the device. Remember to use Backup & Restore to keep a safe copy of your records.
